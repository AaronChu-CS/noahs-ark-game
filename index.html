About

Noah lives in a world filled with violence, pride, and dishonesty, yet he keeps walking with God.

God calls Noah to build an ark of gopher wood, with rooms inside, three decks, a side door, an upper opening, food stores, and pitch sealing it inside and outside.

The game follows Noah as he gathers supplies, raises the ark frame, prepares rooms, guides clean and unclean animal pairs into place, seals the ark, loads the final stores, and enters with his family as the journey begins.
